If a bookmaker offers you 2.50 odds on a team winning, they're essentially saying "for every $1 you bet, you get $2.50 back if you win."
Flipping that around — if the payout is 2.50x your money, the bookmaker is implying the team has a 1 in 2.50 chance of winning, which is 40%.
---
total = sum(raw_probs.values())
vig = (total - 1) * 100

Adds up all the implied probabilities. 
As we know they sum to something like 1.043 instead of 1.0. 
Subtracting 1 and multiplying by 100 converts that excess into a readable percentage, so 1.043 becomes 4.3% vig
